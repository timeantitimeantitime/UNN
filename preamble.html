Preamble

We, the Network States and Societies, united in our commitment to digital sovereignty, decentralized governance, and the collective advancement of human civilization in the digital age,

Recognizing the evolving nature of statehood in the twenty-first century, where network-based communities transcend traditional geographic boundaries and establish legitimate governance structures through technological innovation and social consensus,

Affirming that every Network State and Society possesses inherent rights to self-determination, digital autonomy, and equal representation in the supranational community,

Determined to foster cooperation, mutual recognition, and peaceful coexistence among all network-based political entities,

Have agreed to establish The United Network Nations as the premier international organization dedicated to the recognition, coordination, and advancement of Network States and Societies worldwide.
