Member States and Societies

The United Network Nations currently recognizes the following Network States and Societies as full members:

The Network State of Próspera

Founded: 2021

Location: Roatán, Honduras

Population: ~1,500 residents

Focus: Special Economic Zone with innovative governance

The Network Society of Balancer

Founded: 2022

Location: Decentralized

Population: Global community

Focus: Decentralized finance and governance

The Network State of Cabin

Founded: 2021

Location: Physical nodes worldwide

Population: ~3,000 members

Focus: Creator community with physical meetups

The Network Society of CityDAO

Founded: 2021

Location: Wyoming, USA

Population: ~5,000 citizens

Focus: Community-owned land governance

The Network State of Zuzalu

Founded: 2023

Location: Montenegro (temporary)

Population: ~200 residents

Focus: Experimental community for longevity and innovation

Become a Member

Network States and Societies seeking recognition must demonstrate established governance structures, a defined citizenry, and commitment to the principles outlined in the UNN Preamble.

For membership inquiries, please contact the Secretariat.
